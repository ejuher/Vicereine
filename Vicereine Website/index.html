Q: WHAT DOES VICEREINE MEAN?

Vicereine literally translates to
VICE - "in place of"
REINE - "queen".
a viceroy/vicereine is a representative of a monarch/sovereign who rules over a kingdom or territory by proxy. we like the sound of it.
